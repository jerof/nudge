**Why:** Scrolling fatigue. Mix lengths to maintain attention.

## Resources & Best Practices

### What Works on X
- Personal narratives over product pitches
- Specific numbers over vague claims
- Story arcs over feature lists
- Invitations over demands
- Honesty over perfection
- Building with community over building for users

### Thread Length
- Optimal: 7-11 tweets (not too short, not exhausting)
- Sweet spot: Problem deep enough to be real, solution clear enough to intrigue

### Timing
- Post during peak hours (9am-12pm, 5pm-7pm timezone)
- Let thread run before retweeting (24 hours minimum)
- Engage with replies early (signals to algorithm)

### Engagement Hooks
- "What's your biggest productivity gap?" (mid-thread)
- "Try it and tell me what breaks" (CTA)
- "What would you build if you had the time?" (ending invitation)
